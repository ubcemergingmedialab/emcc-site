Tomorrow's Technology Solving Today's Problems
 

The Challenge

EM2C is not your average hackathon - it is the next generation of collaborative problem-solving events. Over the course of 48 hours, participants will implement emerging media to create tools that enhance teaching & learning. We believe that only through interdisciplinary collaboration can the future of emerging media truly shine. Students from all fields of study are welcome to participate! This event is organized by UBC's Emerging Media Lab and BC Game Jam organizers, BUS.

Event Information


Where: UBC Life Sciences Centre | 2350 Health Sciences Mall
Who: All students, All disciplines
Cost: $10 | Get Tickets
